Our Programs

Practical Ways We Expand Access to Care

Four connected programs work together to help Arkansans reach outpatient mental health care, whatever their circumstances.

Program 01

Counseling Voucher Program

The Counseling Voucher Program provides financial assistance to eligible individuals who cannot afford outpatient counseling. When funding is available, vouchers may help offset the cost of counseling services provided by participating licensed mental health professionals.

Our goal is to ensure that finances are not the reason someone goes without needed mental health care.

How It Works

  • Submit a request through our Get Help form
  • Our team reviews eligibility and available funding
  • Approved applicants are matched with a participating provider

Program 02

Graduate Counseling Internship Program

Graduate counseling students complete supervised practicum and internship experiences while providing affordable counseling services to the community. Interns receive high-quality clinical supervision while gaining valuable experience serving diverse populations.

This program simultaneously supports future mental health professionals and expands access to care.

Program 03

Provider Partner Network

We collaborate with licensed counselors, psychologists, marriage and family therapists, clinical social workers, and other behavioral health professionals throughout Arkansas.

Provider partners may accept referrals, participate in the voucher program, supervise interns, and collaborate on improving access to outpatient mental health care.

Program 04

Community Referral Program

When we cannot directly provide services, we help connect individuals with trusted counseling providers and community resources that best fit their needs.

Our goal is to simplify what is often a confusing process and help people find the right care more quickly.
