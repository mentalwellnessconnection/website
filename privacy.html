Privacy Policy

How We Protect Your Information

Last updated: July 2026

Information We Collect

Mental Wellness Connection may collect information you voluntarily provide through our website forms, including but not limited to your name, contact information, and details related to a request for counseling assistance, volunteer interest, internship application, provider partnership, or donation.

How We Use Your Information

Information submitted through our website is used only to:

  • Evaluate and respond to requests for counseling assistance
  • Process volunteer, internship, and provider partnership applications
  • Process and acknowledge donations
  • Communicate with you about our programs and services, where you have opted in

Confidentiality

Information related to requests for counseling assistance is treated as confidential and is shared only with the individuals or partner providers necessary to evaluate and fulfill your request, consistent with applicable law.

Third-Party Forms

Some forms on this website are hosted by third-party services (such as Google Forms or Jotform). Information submitted through these forms is subject to the privacy practices of those providers in addition to this policy.

Cookies and Analytics

This website does not currently use tracking cookies or third-party analytics. If that changes in the future, this policy will be updated to reflect it.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information submitted to us. No method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Choices

You may opt out of email communications at any time by contacting us at info@mentalwellnessconnection.org.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@mentalwellnessconnection.org.
