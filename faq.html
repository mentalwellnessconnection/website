FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the questions we hear most from individuals, providers, students, volunteers, and donors.

General

Yes. Mental Wellness Connection is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization based in Bentonville, Arkansas, serving communities statewide.

We serve individuals and communities throughout Arkansas, with a focus on reducing barriers to outpatient mental health care statewide.

Getting Help

Visit the Get Help page and submit a short request form. Our team reviews eligibility, funding, and provider availability.

If you are in crisis, call or text 988 for the Suicide & Crisis Lifeline, or call 911 if you are in immediate danger. See our Resources page for more.

Partners & Students

Licensed Arkansas behavioral health professionals, community organizations, and referral partners can all apply on the Partner With Us page. There is no cost to join.

Graduate counseling students can apply through the Internships page. Our clinical team follows up about placement and supervision.

Donors & Volunteers

Donations support counseling vouchers, graduate internship supervision, community outreach, referral coordination, and program expansion.

Visit our Volunteer page to see current opportunities and submit an application.
