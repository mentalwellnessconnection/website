Why We Exist

Too many Arkansans go without the care they need.

Financial hardship, high deductibles, limited providers, transportation barriers, and long wait lists keep people from getting help. Mental Wellness Connection was founded to remove those barriers, one connection at a time.

Programs That Open the Door to Care

Four connected programs work together to make outpatient mental health care more reachable for individuals and families across Arkansas.

Counseling Vouchers

How It Works

Getting help is a simple, three-step process.

We know reaching out can feel hard. We've kept the process straightforward so you can focus on your next step, not the paperwork.

Start Your Request
1

Reach out

Submit a short request describing your situation and what kind of support you're looking for.

2

We review your request

Our team looks at available funding, provider capacity, and the best fit for your needs.

3

You get connected

We connect you with a voucher, a graduate counseling intern, or a trusted community referral.
