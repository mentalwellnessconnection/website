# Mental Wellness Connection Website

Official website for Mental Wellness Connection (MWC), an Arkansas 501(c)(3) nonprofit reducing barriers to outpatient mental health care through counseling vouchers, provider partnerships, internships, and community support.

## About MWC

Mental Wellness Connection exists to improve access to affordable outpatient mental health services throughout Arkansas.

Our work focuses on:

- Counseling vouchers
- Graduate counseling internship opportunities
- Provider partnerships
- Community referrals
- Mental health resources
