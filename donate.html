Bring Mental Healthcare Within Reach

Mental Wellness Connection (MWC) is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit based in Bentonville, Arkansas, working to bring mental healthcare within reach by building awareness, encouraging active living, and expanding access to free and low-cost care for individuals and families facing barriers to care.

We work to close the gap between Arkansans who need mental healthcare and the care they can actually reach.

Your generosity helps us expand mental health awareness, create opportunities for community connection, and increase access to meaningful mental health resources and care across Arkansas.

Your Support Matters

Every gift helps Mental Wellness Connection continue building awareness, encouraging active living, and expanding access to care.

Your Impact

What Your Gift Supports

Across Arkansas, many individuals and families face barriers that make outpatient mental healthcare difficult to obtain. Your support helps reduce those barriers.

Awareness

Building mental health awareness of needs, resources, and solutions.

We help individuals and communities better understand mental health, reduce stigma, recognize when support is needed, and connect with meaningful resources.

Active Living

Encouraging movement, healthy habits, and community connection that strengthen mental wellness.

Through community wellness events and activities, we promote active living as an important part of overall mental health and create opportunities for people to connect with others.

Access to Care

Expanding access to free and low-cost mental healthcare.

We reduce financial, geographic, and logistical barriers to outpatient counseling through counseling vouchers, community partnerships, supervised counseling interns, and a growing network of mental health resources.
