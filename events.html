Events

Community Events & Activities

Mental Wellness Connection hosts and takes part in events that build awareness, encourage active living, and bring our community together.

What to Expect

Kinds of Events We Host

As Mental Wellness Connection grows, event details will be added here. Here's the kind of events you can expect to see.

Community Wellness Events

Activities that support movement, healthy habits, and connection with others — part of our Active Living focus area.

Awareness Events

Outreach and education that help reduce stigma and connect people with mental health resources.

Fundraising Events

Community fundraisers that help fund counseling vouchers, intern supervision, and program growth.

Educational Events

Workshops and presentations that build understanding of mental health topics for individuals, families, and community groups.

No events are scheduled yet. Specific dates and details will be posted here as they're confirmed. Want to help plan or host one? Volunteer with us or get in touch.

Want to bring an event to your community?

We're glad to talk with schools, employers, and community groups about hosting an awareness or wellness event together.
