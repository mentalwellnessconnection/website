Our Focus Areas

Awareness. Active Living. Access to Care.

Mental Wellness Connection organizes its work around three connected focus areas that together bring mental healthcare within reach for individuals and families across Arkansas.

Focus Area 01

Awareness

MWC helps individuals and communities better understand mental health through education, resources, assessments, safety planning, and referral networks. Awareness helps reduce stigma, encourages early support, and connects people with the information and resources they need to support their mental well-being.

This focus area includes:

  • Mental health education
  • Resources for individuals and families
  • Validated screening tools, such as the PHQ-4, to help people understand their mental health (screening is not a diagnosis)
  • Safety planning support
  • Referral networks connecting people to appropriate care

Focus Area 02

Active Living

Mental wellness is strengthened through movement, healthy habits, and connection with others. Through community wellness events and activities, we encourage active living as an important part of overall mental health.

This focus area includes:

  • Community wellness events
  • Activities that support healthy habits
  • Opportunities to connect with others in the community

Focus Area 03

Access to Care

MWC connects individuals and families with outpatient mental health services through counseling vouchers, supervised graduate counseling interns, licensed provider partners, and trusted referral resources. By reducing barriers to care, we help more people receive the support they need.

Counseling Vouchers

Financial assistance that helps offset the cost of outpatient counseling for eligible individuals, when funding is available.

Graduate Counseling Interns

Supervised graduate students provide affordable counseling while training as future mental health professionals.
