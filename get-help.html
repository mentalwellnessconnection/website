Three Ways We May Help

Depending on funding and provider availability, you may qualify for:

Counseling Voucher

Financial assistance that helps offset the cost of sessions with a participating licensed provider.

Graduate Counseling Intern

Affordable counseling from a supervised graduate student gaining hands-on clinical experience.

Community Referral

A connection to a trusted provider or community resource that fits your specific needs.
