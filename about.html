About Us

Our Mission, Our Story, Our Values

Mental Wellness Connection exists because too many Arkansans want counseling but can't reach it. Here's why we started, and what drives our work today.

Who We Are

Bringing Mental Healthcare Within Reach

Mental Wellness Connection is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit based in Bentonville, Arkansas, working to make outpatient mental health care easier to reach for individuals and families across the state. Our work is organized around three focus areas — Awareness, Active Living, and Access to Care — described in more detail on our Focus Areas page.

Mission

Awareness. Active Living. Access to Care.

Mental Wellness Connection brings mental healthcare within reach by building awareness of mental health needs, resources, and solutions; encouraging active living and community connection; and expanding access to free and low-cost outpatient mental healthcare for individuals and families facing barriers to care.

Vision

An Arkansas where care is within reach

We envision an Arkansas where every individual has timely access to compassionate, quality mental health care — regardless of income, insurance status, or geographic location.

Our Story

Built from years inside Arkansas's behavioral health system

Mental Wellness Connection was founded after years of working directly within Arkansas's behavioral healthcare system. Every day, we saw people who wanted counseling but could not afford it. Others had insurance with deductibles that made treatment financially impossible. Rural communities often struggled to find available providers, while counseling graduate students needed quality internship placements to complete their education.

Rather than accepting these barriers as inevitable, we created Mental Wellness Connection to bring providers, students, donors, volunteers, and community organizations together around one goal: making mental health care more accessible.

Our work focuses on creating practical solutions that connect people with care when they need it most.
