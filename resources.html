Insurance Information

Understanding Your Coverage

Many insurance plans cover outpatient mental health services, but deductibles and copays vary widely. Before your first appointment, it can help to ask your insurer:

  • Is outpatient mental health counseling covered under my plan?
  • What is my deductible, and has it been met?
  • Do I need a referral from a primary care provider?
  • Which providers in my area are in-network?
